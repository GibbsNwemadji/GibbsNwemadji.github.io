Research

My current research focuses on the theoretical understanding of learning dynamics in neural networks, with particular interest in scaling laws, superposition, Low-Rank Adaptation fine-tuning, and teacher-student models.

I am currently visiting Yale University, where I work on understanding how superposition can induce scaling laws in deep neural networks, and on the representations induced by LoRA fine-tuning in large language models.
