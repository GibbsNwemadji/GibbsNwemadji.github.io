Research

Theory-driven machine learning with a statistical physics viewpoint.

Learning dynamics and LoRA fine-tuning

I study how pre-training shapes downstream adaptation, with a particular interest in Low-Rank Adaptation and dynamical mechanisms that can help or hurt fine-tuning.

Scaling laws and superposition

I am interested in whether scaling laws can emerge from superposition alone, and how feature geometry affects the observed decay of error with model size.

Teacher-student models

I use teacher-student settings to isolate mechanisms of specialization, representation learning, generalization, and approximation in neural networks.

Quantum machine learning

My earlier work includes questions at the intersection of quantum information, quantum algorithms, and learning theory.
